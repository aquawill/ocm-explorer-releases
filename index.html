OCM Explorer

Inspect, debug, and visualise HERE Optimized Client Map (OCM) tile data with an interactive Leaflet.js frontend.

Before you start

  • You need a HERE Platform account with valid credentials.properties
  • Place credentials at ~/.here/credentials.properties (macOS/Linux) or %USERPROFILE%\.here\credentials.properties (Windows)
  • macOS: if blocked by Gatekeeper, right-click the app → Open
  • The app runs a local server and opens your browser automatically
